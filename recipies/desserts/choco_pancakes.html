  • Chocolate Pancakes

    Type of Food: Dessert

    Tags: #dessert #chocolate #pancake #american #light

    Veg

    Uploaded by @Developer

Ingridients Required:

  • 1¼ cup of All Purpose Flour
  • ¼ cup of Cocoa Powder
  • ¼ cup of Powdered Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tbsp Baking Soda
  • A pinch of Salt
  • 1¼ cup of Warm Milk
  • 2 tbsp of Water
  • Desired Toppings



Steps for Preparation:

  • In a bowl, take ¼ cup of Cocoa Powder, 1¼ cup of All Purpose Flour, ¼ cup of Powdered Sugar, 1 tbsp Baking Powder and Baking Powder with a pinch of Salt and mix it well
  • After mixing, add 1¼ cup of Warm Milk. Add 2 tbsp of Water to the mixture and mix well
  • After mixing, apply some water in a pan and pour a little of the mixture and flip it over after 2-3 bubbles appear. Do the same with the remaining of the mixture.
    Note: Make sure you do not use butter!
  • After it is done, put the toppings of your desire and it is ready to be served!
